Managing emotion is very important

Anger is a natural and mostly automatic response to pain of one form or another. Anger can occur when people don't feel well, feel rejected, feel threatened, or experience some loss. Angry people always feel that their anger is justified. However, other people don't always agree. The social judgment of anger creates real consequences for the angry person.
